While I agree GLP-1 RAs should be high on the list in terms of efficacy, I have a hard time talking patients into daily or even weekly injections. Similarly, SGLT-2 inhibitors come with unattractive genitourinary side effects. My second choice after metformin is normally a DPP-4 inhibitor, such as sitagliptin. I agree that sulfonylureas should now be bottom of the list given the plethora of other options.
